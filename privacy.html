Papageno privacy policy

None of the data recorded by Papageno is personally identifyable.

All this data is stored only on your device. Papageno does not share any of this data, even with the developer, unless explicitly asking you to (for example, in the case of crash reports).

None of this data is ever shared with third parties.

The app does not use any cookies or other tracking functionality.

If you want to erase all data stored by the app, you can do so through the settings of your operating system (Android or iOS).

If you have any questions or concerns, please get in touch with thomas@papageno.app.
